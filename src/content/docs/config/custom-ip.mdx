For selective plans, you can add up to 2 freely chosen IP address or subnet.

The maximum range allowed is `/24` for IPv4 and `/64` for IPv6.

## Add procedure

<Steps>

1. Click on the `+` sign!

	![](/assets/custom-ip1.png)

2. In the window that pops up, click on the `Add IP` button, then type in the IP or subnet you want!

	![](/assets/custom-ip2.png)

3. After pressing the `Save IPs` button, the IP address(es) or subnet(s) will be added immediately.

</Steps>

:::note

On rare occasions you may see a rotating icon in your configuration, this is normal and indicates that your request is being processed!

If your configuration is not active after 5-10 seconds, contact us on Discord or via the **kapcsolat@fxtelekom.org** email address!

:::
