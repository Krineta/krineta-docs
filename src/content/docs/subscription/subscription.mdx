Once you have chosen which package you want, all you have to do is activate it.

## Subscription Procedure

<Steps>

1. On the `Subscriptions` page, click on the `Subscribe` button for the plan of your choice!

	![](/assets/subscribe1.png)

2. You will get a summary, click on the `Activate` button to confirm your choice.

	![](/assets/subscribe2.png)

3. The plan will be activated immediately. You can see the status of your subscription in its description, or in the `Your Subscriptions` section below it.

	![](/assets/subscribe3.png)

</Steps>
