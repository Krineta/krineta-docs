---
title: Track Subscriptions
---

There are several ways to track your subscription and top-ups.

## Dashboard

You can always see your latest transaction on the main page.

![](/assets/track1.png)

## Subscriptions

On the 'Subscriptions' page, in the 'Your Subscriptions' section, you can view the status of your active subscription, including its price and when the active period ends (i.e. when it will be renewed).

![](/assets/track2.png)

## Payment History

On the `Payment History` page, you can see all your payments and transactions.

![](/assets/track3.png)

## Payment Reminders

If you turned on [payment reminders](../../login-flow/register#settings), you will always receive an email if your account needs a recharge before a subscription renew.
