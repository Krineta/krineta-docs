## Login Process

<Steps>

1. Go to the [login](https://fxtelekom.org/login) page!

	![](/assets/login.png)

2. Clicking the 'Continue with Discord' button will redirect you to the Discord login page. If you are already logged in to Discord via your browser, you will be taken directly to the main page.

	![](/assets/dashboard.png)

</Steps>
